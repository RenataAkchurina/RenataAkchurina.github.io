Tamagoyaki

Updated March 28, 2017

Tamagoyaki is a type of Japanese omelette, which is made by rolling together several layers of cooked egg. This recipe is not original, just my own fast and easy way to cook it.

Ingredients: 4 eggs; 5g soya sauce; 1 tablespoon sunflower oil; 1 teaspoon sugar.

Break the eggs into the big dish, add soy sauce and sugar, blend all ingredients with fork until smooth. Heat the frying pan with oil until hot and pour the 1/3 part of mixture into hot pan. Roast until it starts dry, around 2-5 minutes. Quickly roll up thin omelette layer and move it aside where you will start to roll up the next layer. Pour the second part of the mixture into the pan, in such a way that mixture flows under the roasted roll, you can make it with spatula. Roast until it starts drying and roll it up along with the first roll. Cook the third part of mixture in the same way. Slice the omelette into 5-6 pieces, add some herbs (optional) and serve it.
