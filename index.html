Welcome to your source of inspiration and craftsmanship in the world of knitting! Discover new ideas, master new techniques, and create with me!

Hi all! My name is Renata and I have been knitting for approximately 8 years. My first experience was a lace shawl that I knitted with socks yarn, then I tried to crochet and to knit a pullover with a pattern taken from a Magazine.

Hand made clothes are not a trend for me. My mother is a professional tailor and I grew up among well designed and high quality things, which I could use for a long time. I watched the creative process — it was like a magic. Women with different body shapes that need better fitting than manufactured clothes can offer are her typical clients.

I don't know why, but tailoring didn't make me interested like knitting. When I knit according to my own design, I feel free from manufactured products, invasive fashion trends and size guides. Clothes should have individual fitting and emphasize beauty of their owners. I think if the clothing has been produced with high quality and with high attention to the personal needs, it will last for a long time.

How does it look?

The pattern is presented as a PDF file in English, providing a step-by-step guide to the knitting process. It includes detailed photos, video tutorials, diagrams, and recommendations for selecting tools, yarn, and estimating yarn usage. Most of the patterns are designed for sizes XS-L, and this information is specified in the product description.
